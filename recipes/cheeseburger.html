Classic, Juicy Cheese Burger

Our Classic, Juicy Cheese Burger is all about comfort and flavor. A thick beef patty is grilled until it is tender and full of flavor, then topped with a generous slice of cheese that melts perfectly over the meat. Every bite is hearty and satisfying, just the way a good burger should be.

We serve it on a soft toasted bun with crisp lettuce, fresh tomato, sweet red onions, and a few pickles for that extra crunch. It is simple, wholesome, and made to remind you of the kind of burger you would enjoy at a family bbq or around the kitchen table.

Ingredients

  • Freshly ground beef patty
  • Melted cheddar cheese (or your preferred cheese)
  • Soft toasted burger bun
  • Crisp lettuce
  • Sliced tomato
  • Sweet red onions
  • Pickles
  • Mayo (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper
